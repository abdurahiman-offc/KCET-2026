About KCET 2026

KCET is conducted by the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) for admission into Engineering, Architecture, Pharmacy, Agriculture and allied courses in Karnataka.

All applications, admit cards, results and counselling are available only on the official KEA portal.
